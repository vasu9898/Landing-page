About KARE

Kalasalingam Academy of Research and Education (KARE) formerly Arulmigu Kalasalingam College of Engineering, was established in 1984 by the pioneering Kalasalingam Anandam Ammal Charities. KARE was re-accredited by NAAC with ‘A’ grade with a CGPA of 3.11 in 2015. In the recently announced NIRF Rankings 2021, KARE achieved 35th rank in the University category, 39th in Engineering and 50th overall.
